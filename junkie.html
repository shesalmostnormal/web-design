Junkie

Adrenal glands keep convulsing

And hands became damp, shaking

As epinephrine starts to swell

Vasoconstricting; stop and breathe.



The rush builds 'til all is surreal

Like drugs, trust me. It's worth the thrill.

As an amateur, I still fear

The consequences, but I've got skill.



No one will know, unless I'm caught.

Nobody would suspect, but if I fuck

It up--no second thoughts; a five-

Finger discount saves a few bucks.



And my heart speeds at the rate of

Engines. "Hey you!" Someone calls out.

Then the shock channels through my brain

While the blood blitz to my neck's veins.



My pace hastens along with my

Pulse. A hand clutches my shoulder--

I spin around. "You dropped this," says

A mall cop, handing me a ten.
